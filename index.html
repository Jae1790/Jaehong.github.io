Ideal Storage Conditions

To keep your perfume fresh and long-lasting, storage matters!

Why It Matters

Storage conditions affect how long your scent stays beautiful.

Keep it cool & dark

Heat and light break down fragrance molecules. Always store your perfume in a drawer or box.

Avoid humidity

Don°Øt leave perfumes in your bathroom. Humidity causes chemical reactions that change the scent over time.

Good storage = long-lasting scent ?

Extend the Shelf Life of Perfume

Preserving your perfume is all about how you treat it!

Simple habits, long-lasting scents

Follow these practical tips to ensure your perfume stays fresh for years.

Seal it tightly

Always close the cap securely. Exposure to air speeds up evaporation and degrades the scent.

Keep the original box

The box protects against light, temperature changes, and damage. It°Øs not just for decoration!

Take care of your perfume, and it will take care of your style ?
